# PV CAPEX — Lazard LCOE+ 2024 reference

**Scope:** utility-scale ground-mount PV, turnkey installed cost (modules + inverters + trackers + BOS + engineering + land prep). Exclude land acquisition, interconnection studies, and financing.

## Key numbers (2024 $)

| Configuration              | Installed CAPEX     | LCOE (unsubsidized) |
|----------------------------|---------------------|---------------------|
| Fixed-tilt, mono-facial    | $0.80 – $0.95 / W   | $28 – $38 / MWh     |
| Single-axis tracker        | $0.90 – $1.05 / W   | $26 – $36 / MWh     |
| Single-axis bifacial       | $0.95 – $1.10 / W   | $24 – $34 / MWh     |

All figures are DC nameplate. For a 5–10 MW BTM project, expect the upper end of the range; small project + inflation adder.

## What's included

* Modules, inverters, trackers, racking, foundations, DC + AC wiring, SCADA, transformers up to the metering point, EPC labor, commissioning, permits.

## What's excluded (budget separately)

* **Land acquisition / lease** — for a WWTP buffer zone with in-kind lease, typically $0.
* **Utility interconnection study + upgrade** — $50k–$300k depending on complexity.
* **Environmental review** (NEPA / state) — $20k–$100k.
* **Project financing** — varies.

## For the Blog 2 default (30 MGD site, 5.7 MWp single-axis bifacial)

* CAPEX midpoint: **$6.0M** (5,700 kWp × $1.05/W).
* LCOE midpoint: **$29/MWh** unsubsidized.
