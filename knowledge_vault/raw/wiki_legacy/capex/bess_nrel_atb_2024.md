# BESS CAPEX — NREL Annual Technology Baseline 2024

**Scope:** grid-scale Li-ion LFP battery systems, 4-hour duration. Turnkey installed cost at the facility battery DC bus; includes cells, BMS, PCS, thermal management, enclosure, EPC labor, commissioning.

## Key numbers (2024 $, 4-hour duration)

| Component                      | $ / kWh    |
|--------------------------------|-----------|
| Cells (LFP)                    | $95 – $125 |
| Inverter (PCS) + controls      | $55 – $80  |
| Thermal mgmt + fire suppression| $25 – $40  |
| EPC + BoS + commissioning      | $55 – $75  |
| **Total installed**            | **$230 – $320 / kWh**  |

"Conservative ATB" midpoint for 2024: **$275/kWh**.

## Duration scaling

CAPEX scales non-linearly with duration (inverter is fixed per MW, cells scale per MWh).

* 2-hour: ~$450/kWh (high inverter share)
* 4-hour: ~$275/kWh (sweet spot)
* 6-hour: ~$210/kWh
* 8-hour: ~$180/kWh

## Augmentation (lifecycle cost)

Li-ion capacity fades ~2.5%/year. After 10 years, a 4-hour BESS is effectively a 3-hour system at original nameplate. Industry practice: budget **~$40/kWh for augmentation** at year 10 (replacing ~20% of cells). Financially, augmentation adds ~$5–8/kWh amortized over 20 years.

## For the Blog 2 default (8 MWh, 4-hour)

* Nameplate CAPEX: **$2.2M** (8,000 kWh × $275/kWh).
* With augmentation reserve: **$2.5M total lifecycle**.
