# EnergyFlux Knowledge Vault

A persistent, compounding knowledge base for the EnergyFlux Blog 2 demo,
built on Andrej Karpathy's *LLM Wiki* pattern (April 2026).

## Layout

- **`raw/`** holds immutable source documents (papers, articles, data,
  assets, plus a `wiki_legacy/` snapshot of the earlier simplified RAG
  corpus).
- **`wiki/`** holds the synthesized pages — entities, concepts,
  individual sources, blocks, graphs, and synthesis notes.
- **`AGENTS.md`** documents the governance pattern that the chat copilot
  is told to follow.

## Authority levels

Every wiki page declares one of four authority levels:

- `authoritative` — external primary source (NREL ATB, NVIDIA datasheet,
  EPA dataset, peer-reviewed paper). Cited as fact.
- `reviewed` — human-approved synthesis. A senior engineer has read the
  page, checked its citations, and signed off. Cited as fact.
- `candidate` — LLM-drafted or unreviewed synthesis. May be used as a
  starting point, but cited with a "pending review" label.
- `legacy` — imported from earlier work, not yet re-validated. Same
  treatment as `candidate` until a senior engineer signs off.

## Scope as first-class metadata

Every page also declares what host type, region, equipment, and voltage
level it applies to. Lessons from one regime do not auto-apply to
another — a finding about a 30 MGD WWTP at 12.47 kV is not silently
extended to a 100,000-bbl/day refinery at 138 kV.

## How the demo uses the vault

The Blog 2 chat copilot
([`stage1_5_wwtp_dc/apps/blog2_genai_app_v2.py`](../stage1_5_wwtp_dc/apps/blog2_genai_app_v2.py))
retrieves from this vault via
[`stage1_5_wwtp_dc/design/rag_v2.py`](../stage1_5_wwtp_dc/design/rag_v2.py).
Hits return their authority level alongside the snippet, and the copilot
labels its citations accordingly. The pattern is documented in
[`AGENTS.md`](AGENTS.md).

## Browsing locally

The folder is an Obsidian vault — open it with
[Obsidian](https://obsidian.md) for `[[wikilinks]]`, backlinks, and a
graph view. A pre-rendered HTML version of the same content lives at
[`../wiki/`](../wiki/), generated by
[`../scripts/build_wiki.py`](../scripts/build_wiki.py).
